DRAGON VS TIGER

HOW TO PLAY

Dragon Tiger is an exciting card game where players bet on which side will receive the higher card - Dragon or Tiger. Each round, one card is dealt to the Dragon and one to the Tiger. The side with the higher card wins. Aces are low, and suit doesn't matter. If both sides receive equal cards, it's a tie.
